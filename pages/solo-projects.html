Appliance Tracker (work in progress) will help users to keep track of who is using household appliances in real time, as well as who's next in line to use them.

Users will be able to create accounts with Apple or Google credentials, make groups with other users, and make appliances.

When it's someone's turn to use an appliance, they'll be sent a push notification.
